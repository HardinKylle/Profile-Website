September 2024 - July 2025

Led a team of computer science students in the end-to-end development of LigtasMax, a comprehensive thesis project aimed at enhancing disaster response in the City of Manila. As Project Manager, I directed the project lifecycle, from conceptualization to deployment, creating an innovative system that addresses critical gaps in real-time accuracy and communication for disaster management.

  • Spearheaded the design and development of a disaster assistance web and mobile app that enables real-time, community-based reporting for floods and fires.
  • Oversaw the application of the DBSCAN clustering algorithm to analyze user density within affected areas, a key feature that helps emergency services prioritize rescue and aid efforts.
  • Managed the use of Google Firebase for robust backend infrastructure, including real-time database handling and geospatial location tracking.
  • Coordinated the development of a visual dashboard for the GIS Manila Data Hub, enabling effective monitoring and coordination of response efforts.
