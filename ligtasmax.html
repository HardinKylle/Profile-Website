The Challenge

The City of Manila's disaster response was often reactive, lacking real-time, ground-truth data. Emergency services struggled with prioritizing aid due to a lack of accurate information on where the most affected populations were located during floods and fires.

My Contribution

As Project Manager, I led our team through the entire development lifecycle. My specific technical contributions included:

  • Designing and implementing the real-time database schema on Google Firebase.
  • Developing the core logic for the DBSCAN clustering algorithm to analyze user density and identify disaster hotspots.
  • Integrating the Google Maps API for geospatial location tracking and geofencing.
  • Coordinating the development of the visual dashboard for the GIS Manila Data Hub.
