Advocacy

I believe in using technology as a force for positive change. Throughout my career, I've been passionate about supporting causes and initiatives that align with my values and contribute to making the tech industry more inclusive and impactful.

Tech Education for Underserved Communities

I actively support programs that bring coding education and technology resources to underserved communities. By mentoring students and participating in coding workshops, I hope to inspire the next generation of developers and help bridge the digital divide.

Open Source Contribution

I'm a strong advocate for open-source software and believe in the power of collaborative development. I contribute to various open-source projects and encourage others to share their knowledge and code with the community.

Diversity and Inclusion in Tech

Promoting diversity and inclusion within the technology sector is crucial for innovation and progress. I support initiatives that create opportunities for underrepresented groups in tech and work to foster inclusive workplace cultures.

Sustainable Technology Practices

As technology continues to evolve, it's important to consider its environmental impact. I advocate for sustainable coding practices, energy-efficient solutions, and responsible technology use that minimizes our carbon footprint.

Mental Health Awareness in Tech

The tech industry can be demanding, and mental health is often overlooked. I support initiatives that promote work-life balance, mental health resources, and creating supportive environments for developers and tech professionals.
